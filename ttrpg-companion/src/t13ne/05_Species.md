# Alien Species

*   **Humans:** Varied skin/hair/eye colors based on home planets.
*   **Theriocephalics:** Human/Animal DNA hybrids. Smart, engineered for specific planets.
*   **Drones:** Robots/AIs built by humans. Some splinter species exist.
*   **The First:** Primordial, extinct race. Seeded other species. 8 limbs, 5m tall (spider/octopus like).
*   **Draco:** Uplifted dinosaurs created by The First. Caste-based society.
*   **Kathorri:** Made of frozen ice, liquid alcohol, and superconductors. Communicate via EM waves. Hive-mind tendencies.
*   **Vemlek:** Artificial species (robots/computers). Split into Vemleki (anti-biological) and Vemleko (pro-biological).
*   **Grenk:** Biological servitor race of The First. Robust, good memory, not highly intelligent.
*   **Thf-Thf:** Sentient gas/nano-neural hive mind. Often live in "suits" (bubbles). Communicate electromagnetically.
*   **Procedural:** Other species generated programmatically.

While these species were included as examples they are not intended to be the only species in the galaxy (nor even necessarily common species - although I suspect humans should be quite wide spread if not actually common), I would like it if the system was more likely to create and evolve its own species of similar complexity to these.
