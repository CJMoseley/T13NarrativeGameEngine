# Wormhole Racers Controls

Welcome to the Wormhole! To survive the high-speed races through the galaxy, you'll need to master your ship's controls.

## Control Scheme

| Action | Key | Description |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| **Roll-forwards** | `W` | Accelerate forward through the tunnel. Usually by rolling the shield forwards. |
| **Slow Down** | `S` | Slow down or stabilize your speed. |
| **Roll Left** | `A` | Rotate your ship's shield counter-clockwise around the tunnel walls. |
| **Roll Right** | `D` | Rotate your ship's shield clockwise around the tunnel walls. |
| **Slide / Jump** | `<SPACE>` | **Hold** to slide. **Release** to perform a jump! |
| **Brake** | `S` | Slow down or stabilize your speed. |
