# T13 Yarn-Teller's Guide

As a **Yarn-Teller**, you focus on the cards, the flow of narrative, and the interpretation of results.

## The Yarn System
- **Yarn Cards**: Every card has a Pip value (1-13) and a narrative meaning.
- **Card Spreads**: Plots are structured using spreads. A "Story" spread typically contains Frame (Beginning), Loom (Middle), and Zenith (Climax) acts.
- **Interpretation**: Your role is to interpret the card meanings in context. The Engine's AIService can assist by generating evocative summaries of spreads.

## Interaction
- You play cards from the Plot's hand to create "Hooks", "Warps" (conflict), and "Wefts" (recovery).
- Work with the Referee to ensure mechanical results (dice) are reflected in the Yarn cards being played.
