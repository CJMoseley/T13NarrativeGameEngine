# T13 Hero's Player Guide

A **Hero** is a detailed character with significant agency and a complex "Facet Web".

## Hero Structure
- **Facet Web**: You have 24 Facets arranged in 12 pairs. Your stats are balanced; raising one facet usually lowers its anti-facet.
- **Significator**: A unique card or ability that defines your character's theme and grants bonuses when its suit is drawn.
- **Character Arcs**: Heroes have personal arcs (Solo, Duet, Trio). Advancing your arc grants significant Yarn and Chi.

## Advanced Mechanics
- **Strains and Shocks**: You can push yourself by taking Stress (Strains) to gain extra dice. Beware of Shocks, which can cause permanent Traumas.
- **Alternates**: Some "Full" Heroes have alternates in different dimensions. You can swap between them under specific narrative conditions.
