# T13 Standard Player's Guide

Welcome to the **Terminal Thirteen (T13)** Narrative Engine. As a player, you are a co-author of a living story.

## Getting Started
1. **Connect**: Enter a Room Name in the Session Connection panel and click **Join Session**.
2. **Character**: Go to the **Character Sheet** tab. You can view your current stats or create a new "Catalyst".
3. **The Loom**: Watch the **Narrative Plot** tab. This is where the story unfolds. Aces, Queens, and other Yarn cards will appear here as the Referee or the Engine drives the plot.

## Playing the Game
- **Tests**: When the Referee asks for a Test, you will use your Facets and Proficiencies.
- **Sway**: Accumulate Yin and Yang to generate Chi. Chi allows you to buy new Proficiencies and influence the world.
- **Narrative Weaving**: Your character is "hooked" into plots. Your actions influence the tension level and the ultimate resolution of the story.

For more details, see the [Core Concepts](../../rulebook/index.html#core-concepts) in the Rules Handbook.
