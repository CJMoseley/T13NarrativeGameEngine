# T13 Referee's Guide

The **Referee** is the coordinator of the session, managing the technical state, the P2P connection, and the mechanical resolution of Ordeals.

## Hosting a Session
1. Enter a unique Room Name and click **Host Session**.
2. Share the Room Name with your players.
3. Manage VOIP and spatial audio settings in the connection panel.

## Managing Ordeals
- Use the VTT to visualize conflicts.
- Trigger **Tests** (Value, Card, or Dice) when characters face obstacles.
- Resolve Ordeals using **Action Spaces** and **Psychosocial Spaces**.

## The Chronicle
- Monitor the **Narrative Loom** and the **Chronicle** log. The Chronicle records every mechanical and diegetic event, providing a history of the session.
