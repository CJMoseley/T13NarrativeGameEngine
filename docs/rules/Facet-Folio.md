# Facet Folio

T13 like most RPGs abstracts real-world characteristics into something that we model in the game. In the most popular roleplaying games you find six Stats that govern the Character’s Strength, Dexterity, Stamina, Intelligence, Wisdom and Charisma, but then add different mechanisms to determine how lawful, chaotic, good or evil a Character may be. In T13, everything about a Character can be extracted into the Character "Stats", but to do this we require more than six Stats.

T13 uses 24 "Stats" (although they are bound together to make opposed Pairs) that are known as Facets (as in the facets of the character) each Facet has a recognised Anti-Facet that opposes it, and for most Characters their rating in a Facet negatively impacts their rating in the Anti-Facet, automatically providing balance for high powered Stats. A Character with a 16 Boon in Awe is limited to a 10 Boon in Jeer, so they are a little more likely to intimidate or impress someone, than make them laugh.

**Optional Rule:** In some games, in order to make them more unique, or support a particular setting better, some Facets may have different Anti-Facets, for example, the Author may have decided that in their game, which features the spider-trickester god Anansi, that Gossamer is the opposite of Orthodox and Heresy the opposite of Burden. In such a setting, physical strength and knowledge of truth are opposed, one either goes to the library or the gym, and a Character is either comfortably well-off, or a liar or fantasist. Such changes can have profound effects on how the game plays, and how the world of the game feels during play. Even more extreme changes can be made, where a Facet changes from Yin to Yang, for example if Wyrd became a Yang Facet opposed by Yin Jeer, the the Yang Liberty may be opposed by a Yin Awe. This would create a world where Chaos and Law are not opposed, but the laws of mgaic and man are allied with dreams against the character&apos;s inner emotional turmoils.

## Facet List


### Facet List

| Index | Facet | Initial | Alignment | Opposite (Anti-Facet) | Description |
| :---: | :--- | :---: | :---: | :--- | :--- |
| 0 | **Awe** | A | Yang (Active) | Jeer | Awe is the Yang Facet of the sublime and the spirit, it governs emotions and emotional effects. From veneration, to dread, and wonderment to esteem. It is the Facet of Idols and has the emotional maturity to rise above the derision of its opposite of Jeer. |
| 1 | **Burden** | B | Yin (Receptive) | Gossamer | Burden is the Yin Facet of the material. Wealth and weight fall beneath Burden’s corporeal control. It is the Facet of Elemental Earth, passive and receptive it governs the emotions of sadness and trust. It is the opposite of Gossamer. |
| 2 | **Craft** | C | Yang (Active) | Nature | Craft is the Yang Facet of artifice and creation. The Yang opposite of Nature it is the creator that builds, constructs and works. |
| 3 | **Dominion** | D | Yang (Active) | Yonder | Dominion is the Yang Facet of control, groups, civilization, the body politic, the abilities to socialise and lead. It is an active facet that governs groups, areas and control and it is the opposite of Yonder. |
| 4 | **Enigma** | E | Yin (Receptive) | Key | Enigma is the Facet of secrets, mysteries, hidden behaviours and cryptic things. Passive and Yin it is the occult opposite of Key. It is interesting to note that while Enigma is passive, the act of initially hiding is always Active. |
| 5 | **Fury** | F | Yang (Active) | Quiet | Fury is the Yang Facet of anger and deep emotional intensity. It is the unbridled storm tossed sea and the thundering waterfall, all the active dynamic forces are in some way, some of the time governed by its certainty. It’s opposite is the Yin facet Quiet. |
| 6 | **Gossamer** | G | Yang (Active) | Burden | Gossamer is the dynamic Yang Facet of strength without weight. Silken threads drifting in the wind, the forces that bend and shake trees and can rip and tear flesh. Gossamer is the light, airy opposite of Burden. |
| 7 | **Heresy** | H | Yang (Active) | Orthodox | Heresy is the Yang Facet of lies, deceit, fiction and those things believed to be false. It is the governor of error, the ancestor of dissent and the blame for impiety. It is the dynamic opposite of Orthodox. |
| 8 | **Inertia** | I | Yin (Receptive) | Zeal | Inertia is the Yin Facet of time, motion, inexorable momentum, falling and sliding. The mother of indolence and lassitude, the patron of procrastination and patience, this receptive facet will out-wait and out-last its opposite Zeal. |
| 9 | **Jeer** | J | Yin (Receptive) | Awe | Jeer is the Yin Facet of emotions. It is the chuckling king of fools, the master of mimicry and mockery, the home of humour and is of course the joke behind its opposite Awe. |
| 10 | **Key** | K | Yang (Active) | Enigma | Key is the Yang Facet of solutions, answers, ingress and interpretation. It is the patron of perception and penetration, the source of science and the ’key’ to the cipher of its opposite Enigma. It is interesting to note that while Key is Yang Facet and unlocking something is always active, perception is often used passively. |
| 11 | **Liberty** | L | Yang (Active) | Wyrd | Liberty is the Yang Facet of freedom. It is the dream that is imagined, and the imagination itself, the freedom to dream, the license to leisure, the choice of chance, the autocrat of luck and the flexibility and flux to deny its opposite Wyrd. |
| 12 | **Miasma** | M | Yin (Receptive) | Phoenix | Miasma is the Yin Facet of transformation, usually in the form of bad smells, rotting and corruption. The transformative powers are those of potions, poultices, and drugs, it is the power that makes cheese from milk. It is the foulness within, the stink without, it is the dark haze that hides the blight of contamination. So foul is the Facet, that its stench may even survive in the ashes of the purifying fire of Phoenix |
| 13 | **Nature** | N | Yin (Receptive) | Craft | Nature is the Yin Facet of the receptive and nurturing mother of us all. Passive growth, fertility and flesh are all aspects of this Facet. Nature governs all things native, and genuine unlike the artifice of its opposite Craft. |
| 14 | **Orthodox** | O | Yin (Receptive) | Heresy | Orthodox is the Yin Facet of truth. Orthodox is passive data, a simple truth stood in plain sight, it is approved knowledge, confirmed and conventional. It is the latent law, ever present, plain and proper and only occasionally is it stirred into reactionary activity by its opposite Heresy. |
| 15 | **Phoenix** | P | Yang (Active) | Miasma | Phoenix is the Yang Facet of Elemental Fire. Transcendent, the Facet is not just the father of the physical flame, but also the fires within, it is the purveyor of passions and the root of regeneration, its process powers all life and even the stars. Unchecked it can burn and fry all things including its opposite Miasma. |
| 16 | **Quiet** | Q | Yin (Receptive) | Fury | Quiet is the Yin Facet of receptive silence. In that silence rest quiescent woods, tranquil trees, hushed leaves and even the silent speech of books and plain libraries (and mute, conservative librarians). Sober, calm and studied Quiet is the only way to soothe its opposite Fury. |
| 17 | **Rook** | R | Yin (Receptive) | Trial | Rook is the Yin Facet of defence. Receptive and resilient it is the bastion of the Facets. Armour and Aegis, shielding and protective it is the only true guard against its opposite Trial. |
| 18 | **Sin** | S | Yang (Active) | Virtue | Sin is the Yang Facet of vices. It is the source of all evil, the inception of immorality, the well of wickedness and is the Facet of transgression and trespass and the one weakness of its opposite Virtue. |
| 19 | **Trial** | T | Yang (Active) | Rook | Trial is the Yang Facet of competition. It is the commencement of hostilities, the cause of war and the source of sports. Whether striving at tournament or struggling to win Trial is continually in conflict with its opposite Rook. |
| 20 | **Virtue** | V | Yin (Receptive) | Sin | Virtue is the Yin Facet that governs Goodness. The Facet is the home of hope, love and charity. Incorruptible and innocent, it is the Facet of worth and worthiness, and the only Facet that has the transformative compassion to embrace its opposite Sin. |
| 21 | **Wyrd** | W | Yin (Receptive) | Liberty | Wyrd is the Yin Facet of the goddess of destiny. Binding and weaving she envelops everything in her nets of karma, kismet and doom. Her’s is the Facet of inevitability, predestination, misfortune and providence, the only Facet able to plan for and predict its opposite Liberty. |
| 22 | **Yonder** | Y | Yin (Receptive) | Dominion | Yonder is the Yin Facet of space and distance. Completely passively this Facet sits holding point A apart from point B. The decider of distance and the root of range and roads, the Facet oversees all paths from space-lanes to highways to the Lea. Passive and inert Yonder lies always beyond the control but able to reach the heart of its opposite Dominion |
| 23 | **Zeal** | Z | Yang (Active) | Inertia | Zeal is the Yang Facet of dynamic energy and motion. It is the dominant, active faith in oneself or another that energizes enthusiasm, empowers prayers and drives us to our desires. Earnest and eager it is fervour of fanaticism, faith and confidence of belief. Zeal is the only Facet capable of sparking any action in its opposite of Inertia. |

Note that even more extreme changes can be made, where a Facet changes from Yin to Yang, for example if Wyrd became a Yang Facet opposed by Yin Jeer, the the Yang Liberty may be opposed by a Yin Awe.
